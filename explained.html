dooplicator is the easiest way to duplicate your snowmobile, all-terrain vehicle, and personal watercraft key!




RF DESS keys contain a wireless chip checked by the vehicle's electronics module (MPEM/ECU)

the dooplicator iPhone and Android app uses the NFC feature in your mobile device to read data off your key

when you order a key, a custom wireless chip is programmed and placed inside a housing, with a magnet to activate the key sensor

our housings are 3d printed in yellowknife, and have been tested in extreme temperatures

this service works with ALL RF DESS keys for 2015 to 2022 Bombardier Ski-Doo, Sea-Doo, and Can-Am models
