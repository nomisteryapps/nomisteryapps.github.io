the easiest way to duplicate your snowmobile, all-terrain vehicle, and personal watercraft key!




RF DESS keys contain a wireless chip checked by the vehicle electronics module (MPEM/ECU)

the dooplicator iPhone and Android app uses the NFC feature in your mobile device to read data from your factory key

when you order a dooplicator key, the custom wireless chip inside is programmed to match your factory key

if you scan an unlocked 'performance' key, your dooplicator key will also be unlocked

this service works with ALL RF DESS keys for 2015 to 2022 Bombardier Ski-Doo, Sea-Doo, and Can-Am models
